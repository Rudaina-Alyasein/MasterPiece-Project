Who Are We?

We are a platform dedicated to connecting local libraries with a broader community. Our mission is to help libraries expand their reach, increase their visibility, and enhance their offerings by connecting them with new customers online.

We offer a unique platform where local libraries can showcase their collections, offer products, and connect with a growing network of customers. We believe in collaboration, innovation, and customer satisfaction, and we are constantly working to improve the services we provide to libraries and their patrons.

How We Earn ?

Our platform operates on a commission-based model, ensuring that libraries can list their products with no upfront costs. We take a small commission of 15% on each successful transaction, allowing us to maintain and improve the platform while providing the best service to both libraries and customers.

Libraries receive their earnings directly to their preferred payment method, with transparent reporting and no hidden fees.
