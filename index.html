Welcome to your local hub for all things books and stationery. Our platform allows you to shop from your nearest bookstore and stationery store with ease, helping support local businesses while getting what you need at your convenience.

Learn more

How can we help you?

If you need assistance or have any queries, feel free to explore our Support Center or contact us via email. We're here to help!

To change your billing information, follow these steps:

  • Sign in to your account on our website.
  • Click your profile picture in the top-right corner and select "Account Settings."
  • Under "Billing Information," click "Edit."
  • Update your details and click "Save."

Our payment system securely processes transactions between buyers and sellers using trusted financial institutions. Here’s how it works:
  • Choose your payment method.
  • Confirm your transaction.
  • Wait for your payment to be processed.

To cancel your account:

  • Log into your account.
  • Go to "Account Settings" by clicking your profile picture.
  • Scroll down and click "Cancel Account."
  • Confirm your decision by entering your password.
